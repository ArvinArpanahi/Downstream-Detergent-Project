# Financial Estimation & Investment Scenario

## Overview

This section provides a simplified financial estimation model based on a medium-scale detergent production facility.

The goal is to demonstrate the economic potential and investment attractiveness of the project.

---

## Estimated Initial Investment (CAPEX)

| Category                     | Estimated Cost (USD) |
|----------------------------|---------------------|
| Land and site development  | 500,000 – 1,000,000 |
| Machinery and equipment    | 1,500,000 – 3,000,000 |
| Production line setup      | 500,000 – 1,000,000 |
| Storage and logistics      | 300,000 – 700,000 |
| Working capital            | 500,000 – 1,000,000 |
| Total Investment       | 3M – 6M USD |

---

## Production Capacity (Example Scenario)

- Daily production: 50 – 100 tons  
- Monthly production: 1,500 – 3,000 tons  
- Annual production: 18,000 – 36,000 tons  

---

## Revenue Estimation

Average selling price (export market):

- 500 – 800 USD per ton  

Estimated annual revenue:

- 9M – 28M USD

---

## Profit Estimation

Estimated gross margin:

- 20% – 35%

Estimated annual profit:

- 2M – 8M USD

---

## Return on Investment (ROI)

Estimated ROI period:

- 1.5 – 3 years

---

## Key Financial Advantages

- Low production cost due to location strategy  
- High demand and fast-moving products  
- Export-driven revenue model  
- Scalable production capacity  
- Strong margin potential  

---

## Risk Considerations

- Market price fluctuations  
- Currency exchange risks  
- Supply chain interruptions  
- Regulatory changes  

---

## Strategic Financial Conclusion

This project demonstrates strong financial potential through:

- High revenue capacity  
- Attractive profit margins  
- Fast return on investment  
- Export-driven growth  

It represents a practical and scalable industrial investment opportunity.
