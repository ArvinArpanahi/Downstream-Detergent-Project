# Investment Summary

## Project Overview

This project presents a scalable industrial solution for the production of detergent and cleaning products, strategically positioned to leverage proximity to petrochemical resources and high-demand export markets.

---

## Why This Project?

- Strong and consistent demand for cleaning products  
- Export-oriented business model  
- Strategic location advantage  
- Low production cost structure  
- Scalable industrial design  

---

## Market Opportunity

The target markets include:

- Iraq  
- Gulf countries (UAE, Kuwait, Saudi Arabia, Bahrain)  
- Afghanistan  
- Pakistan  

These regions demonstrate high dependency on imported cleaning products, creating a stable and growing demand.

---

- Estimated Profit: 2M – 8M USD  
- ROI: 1.5 – 3 years
